A one versus one game where players swirl brandy glasses to charge an attack, and launch it to damage the other. There also appears to be some counterattack move, and vulnerability in case both players' blinds are open simultaneously.

{{<0ThXL9I0-zQ>}}

Why did this catch my eye? Blinds as controllers seemed pretty out there, so I wanted to have a closer look how they're used. I found the overall concept to be rather enjoyable, from the storytelling to graphical style. I could feel myself settling into the shoes of a Japanese mob boss, furiously swirling a glass of the finest brandy, while periodically taking peeks through the blinds to inflict my wrath upon my rival.
